HOW TO PLAY!

About the game: Welcome to SnakeSpeare! Snakespeare is a fun and engaging game designed specifically for secondary school students in year 7. The game aims to improve their critical thinking and connection to the non-stem subject English, particularly the works of William Shakespeare. By incorporating a competitive element, the game also enhances knowledge retention by encouraging students to engage with the material in a more active and interactive way. Whether played in the classroom or at home, the game is a valuable tool for educators and parents alike, helping to make learning more fun and engaging for students.

Starting the game: Start by entering your name and school name and submit to be added to the leaderboard. When you have added your name, it's time to play. Click PLAY GAME to start!

Objective: The main goal of the game is to control SnakeSpeare to answer the English question provided. Don't get it wrong otherwise you will loose your highscore! Achieve a high score and show off your critical thinking skills in English by getting to the top of the leaderboard.

Controls: Use arrow keys or "w","a","s","d" to move the snake.

Scoring: Increase your score by answering the the questions correctly and choosing the correct colour that matches the answer!.

Leaderboard: At the end of the game, your score will be submitted to the leaderboard. Compete with your friends and other players to see who can achieve the highest score!
